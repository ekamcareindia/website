About Ekam Care

Why Ekam Care Exists

In emergencies, every second matters. Often, victims are unable to speak, unlock their phones, or explain who to contact. Ekam Care was created to solve this critical gap.

The Ekam Care Emergency Card ensures that any nearby person can help by simply scanning a QR code. Within seconds, family members receive an emergency alert along with the cardholder's real-time location, enabling faster response and immediate action.

Ekam Care is not just a card — it's a life-saving safety accessory.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Ekam Care

How does the Ekam Care card work?

Simply scan the QR code on the card with any smartphone camera. It will open WhatsApp with a pre-filled emergency message and your live location, ready to send to your emergency contacts.

Do I need to install any app?

No app installation required! The card works with any smartphone's built-in camera and WhatsApp, which most people already have installed.

How much does it cost?

Each card costs ₹299 with free first-year support. That's just ₹0.81 per day for complete emergency protection!

Can I update my information later?

Yes! You can update your emergency contacts and personal information anytime for ₹49. We'll update your QR code accordingly.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely! Your data is encrypted and stored securely. The QR code only contains the necessary information to contact your emergency contacts in case of an emergency.

If my card gets stolen or I misplace it, can I block my card?

Yes, we can block the card and it will be free of cost as safety is our priority and it goes hand in hand with privacy.
